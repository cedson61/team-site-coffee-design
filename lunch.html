Menu

Stop in for lunch!

We have a very diverse lunch menu that includes:




    Bacon, Cheese & Egg Breakfast Sandwich- Sizzling Applewood smoked bacon, melty aged cheese and a parmesan frittata are layered on an artisan roll for extra smoky breakfast goodness.
    Oatmeal- Customize your own oatmeal with a variety of toppings
    Sausage, Cheddar & Egg Breakfast Sandwich- a Southern-style sausage patty with a hit of spice, fluffy eggs and aged Cheddar cheese are served on a perfectly toasty English muffin.
    Roasted Ham, Swiss & Egg Breakfast Sandwich- hickory smoked ham and nutty melty Swiss cheese layered on a flaky, all in between a buttery croissant.
