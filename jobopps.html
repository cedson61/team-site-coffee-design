Job Opportunities

Here at Coffee Co, we have many career oppourtinies. Such positions include:

    Barista - a person whose job involves preparing and serving different types of coffee.
    Shift supervisor - responsible for assisting the store manager in maintaining a positive environment
    Shift manager - the responsibility of overseeing a number of subordinate employees during a certain period of time.
    Assistant store manager - Trains store staff
    Store manager - responsible for the day-to-day operations of a retail store
    District manager - a manager who supervises the sales activity for a district.
    Accounting - Prepare, examine, and analyze accounting records, financial statements, and other financial reports to assess accuracy, completeness, and conformance to reporting and procedural standards.
    Finance - management of money
    Legal - make sure everything is legal
    Operations - promoting
    Research and development - developing new foods and drinks
    Technology - Focuses on technological advancements
    CEO - the most senior corporate, executive, or administrative officer in charge of managing an organization
    CFO - is a corporate officer primarily responsible for managing the financial risks of the corporation.

If you feel like you would be interested in working with us, contact us!
